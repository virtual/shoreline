Program Options

University Transfer

Explore our University Transfer options if you wish to continue your studies at a four-year school. If you don't see an area of study you're interested in, contact us. In most cases we can help you prepare for the major of your choice.

Career Advancement

"Professional/Technical" or vocational studies provide students with specialized knowledge and skills to either prepare them to enter a particular field, or enhance existing skills while already employed.

College Readiness

You can also prepare for college-level courses by enrolling in English as a Second Language (ESL), Adult Basic Education (ABE) or General Education Development (GED) courses, or taking part in our high school programs.
